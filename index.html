Top Tips for First-Time Homebuyers

Buying your first home is a major milestone and an exciting adventure. Here are some essential tips to help you navigate the process:

  • Get Pre-Approved: Before you start house hunting, get pre-approved for a mortgage to know how much you can afford.
  • Save for a Down Payment: Aim for a 20% down payment to avoid private mortgage insurance (PMI) and secure better rates. However, if 20% isn't in your budget that is understandable and there are low down payment and even zero down payment options available.
  • Understand Your Credit Score: Your credit score significantly impacts your mortgage rate. Check and improve it if necessary.
  • Consider All Costs: Factor in property taxes, insurance, and maintenance when budgeting for your new home.
  • Work with a Broker: A mortgage broker can offer access to multiple lenders and programs to find the best deal for you.
